Who are we?

Uncommon Digital is doing business as Uniscrow, a universal escrow platform to boost your business.

Uncommon Digital is an Italian startup based in Cagliari and established in 2020. Essentially, we envision a world where people can do business online without barriers. Our founders had the aim of developing solutions and products to facilitate peer-to-peer business relationships. They wanted to make this a reality; hence, they founded Uncommon Digital!
