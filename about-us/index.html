ABOUT US

We are an Italian startup based in Cagliari.

We want to remove geographic barriers and ensure fraud protection and transparency in deal management. In particular, we develop tools and smart legal contracts to protect payments, close deals and prevent disputes. We also strongly believe in decentralization. Adopting the principle of least authority, our solutions make the best use of blockchain and crypto technology. We thus remove any type of intermediary whenever possible.
